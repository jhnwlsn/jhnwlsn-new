Background

Ivy is an online publishing and interactive training platform. Everyone from startups to large enterprises can use Ivy to create their own technical courses. Throughout 2015 and 2016, I designed and developed the marketing site and was a designer for the "player" layer of the platform.

Ivy's main logo

The concept for Ivy was born out of Envy's successes with Code School. Online learning was becoming an important part of the tech industry, and Envy found itself building a similar concept over-and-over again based off of Code School. Thus, creating a reusable platform would decrease client's costs while increase Envy's productivity.

Longform Content

Taking cues from both Code School and several other previous clients, we decided to build the structure for a single course around longform formatted content. By formatting the content into this structure, it limits unneccessary interuptions and helps the learner stay focused and truly learn from what is being communicated.
