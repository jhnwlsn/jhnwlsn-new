About

Howdy! My name is John Wilson. I’m a moonlighting freelance designer and developer who works out of sweltering Orlando. I most enjoy spending time behind my computer by serving smaller businesses and startups. Specifically, I love helping these organizations by designing and creating websites to help promote their products and services. I use a pencil and Sketch for designing and Ruby and/or Javascript for front-end and back-end development.

Work

Throughout the past few years, I have worked with both large enterprise organizations like Cisco as well as smaller organizations like Barna and Ligonier Ministries.

Availability

I tend to work best with quick turnaround projects that last just a couple of weeks. In the spirit of transparency: the most ideal projects are marketing sites with pre-written content, UX/UI redesigns and front-end buildouts. I book my time in 2 week blocks (pre-paid, starting at $1,500).
